Filtering




To view articles based on their political party or lack thereof, hover over the filter icon in the top right corner of the screen. This will trigger a dropdown menu where you will be able to select what kinds of articles you would like to read: right-leaning, left-leaning, or neutral on the political spectrum.

Identifying Political Spectrum




When you hover over an article's associated link, you will notice an image pop up in front of it with a spectrum ranging from dark blue to dark red. This image will tell you where the article you are hovering over is on the political spectrum.
