News Categories


Balanced News offers the following news categories:

  • Top US Headlines (home page)
  • Entertainment
  • Sports
  • Business
  • Science
  • Health

Filtering




To view articles based on their political classification or lack thereof, hover over the filter icon in the top right corner of the screen. This will trigger a dropdown menu that will allow you to select what kinds of articles you would like to read: right-leaning, left-leaning, or neutral on the political spectrum.

Identifying Political Spectrum




When you hover over an article's associated link, you will notice an image pop up in front of it with a spectrum ranging from dark blue to dark red. This image will tell you where the article you are hovering over is on the political spectrum.


Below is a table explaining the meaning of the images you will see:


Spectrum Image

Political Assignment

Moderate Left

*Note: It is likely that political classifications for articles under the entertainment, sports, and science categories will not be as accurate because they tend to involve less political rhetoric.
